We Care.

If you need any help, check out this page. If you don't find what you were looking for, contact us and we'll gladly help!


Can wheelchair users pass through automatic ticket gates?

The ticket gates are built to the latest accessibility standards with at least one extra wide gate to accommodate wheelchairs. As well as serving passengers with disabilities, the gates are designed to ensure passengers with pushchairs, small children or large amounts of luggage are not delayed. Staff will always be on hand to offer assistance.

How do I arrange assistance with boarding and alighting from the train?

We seek to make your journey a pleasant and successful experience and aim to provide all necessary assistance to disabled customers across our network, whether booked in advance or not. We recommend that assistance is requested 24 hours in advance whenever possible to allow us the best opportunity to meet your needs.

I want to book for a group. Who do I contact and are there any discounts?

If you are travelling in a group of ten or more on Greater Anglia services please contact Greater Anglia Group Travel on 0345 600 7245 (select option 5) - open Monday to Friday 08:00 - 18:00.

How do I contact the Greater Anglia Business Travel Service?

Greater Anglia's Sustainable Transport Plans and Carnet Tickets can be purchased through our Business Travel Service

How long have I got to make a claim for refund?

Refunds must be claimed within 28 days of the ticket's expiry date. The expiry date depends on the terms and conditions of the ticket you have purchased.If you have bought your tickets on this website, you can check the terms and conditions of your ticket by logging into My Account and click on the ticket for which you require a refund. The conditions relating to that ticket will be displayed.

What should I do if I have received two identical sets of tickets?

If you only booked one set of tickets and you have received two, please check your bookings in My Account under "Check your order status".If a duplicate booking has been made, log into My Account click on "Apply for a Refund" and follow the instructions.

How do I refund or change my e-ticket?

E-tickets cannot be refunded or changed.

What is an e-ticket?

An e-ticket is a new type of electronic ticket, which we send to you as a PDF file. You can choose to it print yourself or use it on your mobile device. It replaces our older Print Your Own ticket.

How do I buy an e-ticket?

The e-ticket option is available when you buy a Greater Anglia Advance fare online. After completing your purchase, you will receive two emails from us: a booking confirmation email (this is not your ticket) and a second email containing your PDF ticket. You will receive a PDF e-ticket file for each passenger travelling.

How do I collect an e-ticket?

An e-ticket is designed to be printed at home or used on a mobile device. You cannot collect an e-ticket from a station.

What happens if I forget my e-ticket or my mobile device battery runs out?

It’s your responsibility to make sure that you can present the ticket you purchased at the ticket gate and when asked by a member of rail staff. If you can’t – for whatever reason – then you will need to buy a new ticket in order to travel.

Can I buy a ticket for someone else?

Train tickets can be used by anyone even though certain tickets will have the name of the traveller or person who booked the tickets printed on them.When you are making a booking you can request that the tickets are sent to a different UK address.If you do purchase tickets for someone else please note that enclosed with the tickets will be the sales receipt. This shows the last four digits of the credit card as well as the purchase price.

How do I find my booking reference?

When you buy a ticket online you will be given a Ticket Reference number which you need when collecting your tickets.
